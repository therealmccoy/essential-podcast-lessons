About the EPL - Essential Podcast Lessons

March 27, 2018 by abhishek rai

Podcasts have become very popular in last years, reaching millions of listeners daily.

People listen to their favourite podcasts, while commuting, at office or at home; virtually at every concievable place.

While many podcasts have a transcript available to enable jotting down, important points and note taking; still it is a task figure out what is important and what is not.

Many a times, at the first listening, things are not obvious and it requires listening again, for better comprehension.

What if there is a site, where important lessons from top podacsts are listed; so that once you have finished listening to that podcasts, you can refer to this list and may decide to agree or dis-agree with it, copy it or ignore it, debate or just discard the opinions of the list author.

EPL or Essential Podcast Lessons is this one place.

Feel free to reach out to author and also comment on posts.
